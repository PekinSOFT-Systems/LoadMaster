Detailed Description

The ChartModel class defines the Chart of Accounts for Load Master™ load tracking and accounting system for owner/operator truck drivers.

This Chart of Accounts is perfect for all small-business, single-truck owner operators. PekinSOFT™ Systems has defined the following Chart of Accounts, just for you.

Account # Name Short Description
9999 AP Accounts Payable: What you owe others on credit.
10010 Accounting All accounting and legal expenses.
10020 Communications All communications expenses.
10030 Fees Any fees that you need to pay.
10040 Fuel All fuel related expenses.
10050 Insurance All insurance expenses.
10060 Interest All business-related interest expenses.
10070 Loading/Lumping All loader and lumper fees.
10080 Maintenance Maintenance and repair expenses.
10090 Office All office expenses.
10100 Physicals All business-related medical costs.
10110 Rent/Lease All rent/lease fees and expenses.
10120 Scales Scale costs and PrePASS expenses.
10130 Supplies Miscellaneous supplies.
10140 Tax/License Business-related tax/license expenses.
10150 Tolls/Parking Toll charges and parking fees.
10160 Travel Business-related travel expenses.
10170 Uniforms Any uniform or logo shirt expenses.
10180 Wages All payroll expenses.
50010 Checking Account Primary banking account.
50020 Savings Account Primary savings account.
50030 Retirement Savings Account Savings for retirement.
50040 Fuel Card Fuel card account.
50050 Maintenance Reserve Reserve account for maintenance and repairs.
50500 Accounts Receivable Your truck pay at dispatch.

By using the default Chart of Accounts, the one-truck owner/operator is sure to have all of his/her ducks in a row when the tax man comes around.

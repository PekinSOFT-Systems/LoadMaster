Detailed Description

CheckingModel represents the user's checking account.

This Journal, if properly maintained, will not only allow the user to always know how much s/he has in their checking account, s/he will also have a history of where their money came from and where it went.

Developer's Note
This class has gone through many modifications in a very short time. However, the most import thing to note is that the methods isBalanced and setBalanced are now simply overrides to the super.isPosted and super.setPosted, respectively. We determined that, even though checking account transactions do not get posted to the General ledger, there was no sense in having a completely separate class to maintain for the checking account. We simply decided to redirect, so to speak, the balanced field for the checking account to the posted field of a general accounting transaction. All other fields' getters and setters are contained within the super class, BaseTransaction.

\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
{\ttfamily Checking\+Model} represents the user\textquotesingle{}s checking account. 

This Journal, if properly maintained, will not only allow the user to always know how much s/he has in their checking account, s/he will also have a history of where their money came from and where it went.


\begin{DoxyDescription}
\item[Developer\textquotesingle{}s Note]This class has gone through many modifications in a very short time. However, the most import thing to note is that the methods {\ttfamily is\+Balanced} and {\ttfamily set\+Balanced} are now simply overrides to the {\ttfamily super.\+is\+Posted} and {\ttfamily super.\+set\+Posted}, respectively. We determined that, even though checking account transactions do not get posted to the General ledger, there was no sense in having a completely separate class to maintain for the checking account. We simply decided to redirect, so to speak, the balanced field for the checking account to the posted field of a general accounting transaction. All other fields\textquotesingle{} getters and setters are contained within the super class, {\ttfamily Base\+Transaction}.
\end{DoxyDescription}

\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The {\ttfamily Fuel\+Entry\+Model} defines the data required and stored for a fuel purchase made through Load Master. 

This class provides a means for a driver to store critical information about each fuel purchase as a means of maintaining a history of fuel purchases and understanding how driving conditions, speeds, etc., affect fuel economy. By having all of this information stored in the Fuel Account Journal, {\ttfamily 10040.\+jrnl}, the driver will be able to make informed decisions regarding his/her driving practices in order to reduce their fuel expense over time.
